About Liuga Haus Cafe

Welcome to Liuga Haus Cafe, where creativity meets culinary delight. Our cafe is not just about food and drink; it's about community, art, and a passion for quality. Founded in 2024, Liuga Haus has quickly become a beloved spot for locals and visitors alike, offering a warm atmosphere and a menu crafted with love.

Our Mission

At Liuga Haus, our mission is to provide an inviting space where everyone can enjoy top-quality treats while soaking in the unique artistic vibe of our cafe. We believe in using the finest ingredients to create mouth-watering ice creams and pastries that bring joy to every bite.

Meet the Team

Our team is a passionate group of food enthusiasts, artists, and community-minded individuals. We work together to create an experience that goes beyond just a cafe visit — it’s a place where memories are made.
